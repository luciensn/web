Cooking Guide


🍗 Wings
  • Cook ~2 hrs @ 250°F (11:00AM - 1:00PM)
  • Minimum 165°F internal temp
  • Use 1/2 bag of dry rub per case
  • Use top racks to cook fastest

🦃 Turkey
  • Cook ~3 hrs @ 250°F (11:00AM - 2:00PM)
  • Minimum 165°F internal temp
  • Use top racks to cook fastest

🥓 Ribs & Tips
  • Cook ~5 hrs @ 225°F (11:00AM - 4:00PM)
  • Minimum 195°F internal temp
  • Can start cooking at 250°F (with wings & turkey)
  • ⚠️ Turn dial down to 225°F after all wings & turkey done
  • When space becomes available, move ribs up to top racks

🌭 Hotdogs
  • Cook 45min @ 225°F (3:15PM - 4:00PM)
  • Do not overcook

🥩 Pork Shoulder Butts
  • Cook overnight @ 215°F (5:00PM - 9:00AM)
  • ⚠️ Do not use top rack
